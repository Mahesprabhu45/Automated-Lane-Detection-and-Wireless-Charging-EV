Smart Charge Drive - Bill of Materials (BOM)
===========================================

Project: Smart-Charge-Drive
Purpose: Wireless Charging + Automated Lane Guidance EV Prototype

| IR Sensor Modules                  | 2   | Digital Line Following Sensors              |
| DC Gear Motors                     | 2   | 6–12V, high torque                          |
| Motor Driver (H-Bridge/MOSFET)     | 1   | IRFZ44N or equivalent                        |

| LCD Display (16x2, I2C module)     | 1   | Status and voltage display                  |
| Voltage Sensor Module              | 1   | Analog voltage readout                      |
| Li-ion Battery Pack (18650)        | 1   | Rechargeable power storage                  |
| Soldering Wire                     | 1   | For hardware connections                    |
| Jumper/Connecting Wires            | 1 set | Male-Male / Male-Female as required       |
| Push Button Switch                 | 1   | System reset/control input                  |

| Diodes (1N4007 / 1N4148)           | 2–4 | Protection and rectification                |

Notes:
- Coil turn count depends on operating frequency (typically 18–25 turns).
- Ensure heat sinking for 7805 regulator if supply > 9V.
- Replace IR sensors with vision system if future upgrade planned.

Total Estimated Cost: ₹1500 – ₹3500 depending on components and vendor.
